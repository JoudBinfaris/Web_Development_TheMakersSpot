FAQs:

What is The Maker’s Spot?

The Maker’s Spot is a creative hub where you can explore and book hands-on workshops in pottery, knitting, flower arranging, and other crafts — all guided by experienced makers.

Who can join the workshops?

Anyone! Our workshops are open to beginners, hobbyists, and experienced crafters. Each session lists its skill level so you can choose what fits you best.

Where are the workshops held?

Workshops take place at one of our cozy studios, designed to inspire creativity.

Do I need to bring anything?

Just yourself and your creativity! All materials and tools are provided unless otherwise stated in the workshop description.

Will I get to take home what I make?

Yes! Every participant leaves with their handmade creation — whether it’s a ceramic mug, a knitted piece, or a floral arrangement.

What kind of workshops do you offer?

From pottery to flower arranging and knitting, we offer creative sessions for all moods and skill levels. Each workshop is a chance to relax, learn something new, and make something by hand.

How early should I arrive?

We recommend arriving about 10–15 minutes early to settle in, grab a drink, and get comfy before we start.

Who can I talk to if I have a question?

Our friendly team is always happy to help! You can reach us through our email info@TheMakersSpot.com
